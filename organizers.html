Our Mission

We believe that mathematics is a powerful tool for understanding the world and developing critical thinking skills. Through GPHMT, we aim to:

  • Create an inclusive competition that welcomes all high school mathematicians
  • Develop challenging and fair examination questions
  • Recognize and celebrate mathematical achievement
  • Build community among math enthusiasts in the Greater Pacific Highlands region
  • Inspire students to pursue further studies in mathematics and STEM

Tournament Development

Our organizers are committed to continuous improvement. Each year, we:

  • Carefully develop 20 original multiple-choice questions that test a range of skills
  • Conduct thorough testing and validation of all exam materials
  • Gather feedback from participants and teachers
  • Refine our processes to ensure fairness and quality
  • Provide detailed solutions and scoring criteria
