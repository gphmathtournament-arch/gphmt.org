Why Sponsor GPHMT?

Sponsoring GPHMT is a meaningful way to support mathematics education and make a direct impact on student success:

  • Support local students pursuing mathematical excellence
  • Increase your organization's visibility in the community
  • Partner with a well-established educational initiative
  • Make a measurable impact on student achievement
  • Network with community leaders and educators
  • Receive recognition on our website and event materials

Become a Sponsor

Interested in supporting GPHMT? We welcome sponsors at all levels who share our commitment to mathematics education.

We work with each sponsor to create a partnership that works for your organization, whether through financial contributions, in-kind donations, or volunteer support.
