Become a Sponsor

Interested in supporting GPHMT? We offer sponsorship packages at various levels with meaningful recognition for your organization.

Sponsorship Benefits

  • Bronze ($500+): Logo on website and event materials
  • Silver ($1,000+): Bronze benefits plus social media recognition
  • Gold ($2,500+): Silver benefits plus award sponsorship naming rights
  • Platinum ($5,000+): Gold benefits plus prominent event branding and speaking opportunity

Why Sponsor GPHMT?

  • Support local students pursuing mathematical excellence
  • Increase your organization's visibility in the community
  • Partner with a well-established educational initiative
  • Make a measurable impact on student success
  • Network with other community leaders
