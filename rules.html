Code of Conduct

  • Treat all participants, proctors, and staff with respect
  • Maintain academic honesty throughout the competition
  • Follow all proctor instructions without argument
  • No communication between students during the exam
  • No use of unauthorized materials or devices
  • Violations may result in disqualification

Pre-Exam Requirements

  • Arrive at least 15 minutes before start time
  • Bring valid student ID
  • Bring approved writing instruments (pencil or pen)
  • Bring approved calculator (if applicable)
  • Sign in and acknowledge rules

During the Exam

  • No talking or communication allowed
  • No phone or device use permitted
  • Raise hand for proctor assistance only
  • Remain seated for entire exam duration
  • No bathroom breaks once exam begins
  • Proctor decisions are final

Important Notes

These rules are established to ensure fair and equitable competition for all participants. The tournament organizers reserve the right to modify or update rules as needed to maintain the integrity of the competition.

Any questions about rules should be directed to organizers@gphmt.org before the day of the tournament.
