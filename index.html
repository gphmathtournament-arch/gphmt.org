Rounds & Structure

Three rounds designed to test speed, depth, and collaboration.

Individual Round

A written round focusing on creative problem solving in algebra, combinatorics, geometry, and number theory. Problems increase in difficulty and reward persistence.

  • Format: short answer
  • Suggested level: AMC/AIME-style
  • Scoring rewards accuracy over guessing
Duration90 minutes
Questions~20‚Äì25
DifficultyAMC8 ‚Üí AIME

Team Round

Four students, one answer sheet. Problems are designed to encourage discussion, argument, and multiple approaches to the same problem.

  • Teams of up to 4
  • Mixed difficulty, heavy on collaboration
  • Tiebreakers may use team scores
Duration60 minutes
TeamsUp to 4 students
SkillsCommunication, strategy

Guts / Relay Round

Fast-paced, high-energy round where teams receive sets of problems in waves. Perfect for students who enjoy racing the clock.

  • Problems released in sets
  • Score updates during the round
  • Great for live spectating
Duration60 minutes
FormatGuts-style
