About the Tournament

Tournament Format

The Greater Pacific Highlands Math Tournament consists of a single, timed round featuring 20 multiple choice questions. Participants will have [TIMEFRAME TBD] to complete the examination.

Who Can Participate

This tournament is open to high school students from the Greater Pacific Highlands region. Competitors of all skill levels are encouraged to participate and challenge themselves against peers.

Question Difficulty

Questions range from introductory to advanced, testing knowledge of algebra, geometry, trigonometry, and other mathematical topics appropriate for high school level competition.
