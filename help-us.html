Get Involved

GPHMT relies on the support of dedicated volunteers and sponsors to succeed. Whether you can donate your time, resources, or expertise, we'd love to have you on our team!

Volunteer

We're always looking for enthusiastic volunteers to help with various aspects of GPHMT. Whether you're a teacher, student, or math enthusiast, there's a role for you!

Volunteer Opportunities

  • Question Development: Help create and review exam questions
  • Event Proctoring: Supervise exams during the tournament
  • Grading: Score exams and compile results
  • Outreach: Promote GPHMT to schools and students
  • Logistics: Help with event planning and setup
  • Mentoring: Guide students in test preparation

Sponsor

Sponsorship provides essential funding for GPHMT operations, exam development, prizes, and student outreach. We offer various sponsorship levels with recognition benefits.

Sponsorship Levels

  • Bronze Sponsor ($500+): Logo on website and event materials
  • Silver Sponsor ($1,000+): Bronze benefits plus social media mentions
  • Gold Sponsor ($2,500+): Silver benefits plus award sponsorship
  • Platinum Sponsor ($5,000+): Gold benefits plus prominent event recognition

Sponsorship Information

GPHMT is a non-profit initiative dedicated to promoting mathematical excellence. Sponsorship support helps us:

  • Develop high-quality examination questions
  • Provide prizes and recognition for top performers
  • Reach underserved students in our region
  • Host the tournament at accessible venues
  • Offer resources to help students prepare
