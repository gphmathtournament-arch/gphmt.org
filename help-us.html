Get Involved

GPHMT relies on the support of dedicated volunteers and sponsors to succeed. Whether you can donate your time, resources, or expertise, we'd love to have you on our team!

Volunteer

We're always looking for enthusiastic volunteers to help with various aspects of GPHMT. Whether you're a teacher, student, or math enthusiast, there's a role for you!

Volunteer Opportunities

  • Question Development: Help create and review exam questions
  • Event Proctoring: Supervise exams during the tournament
  • Grading: Score exams and compile results
  • Outreach: Promote GPHMT to schools and students
  • Logistics: Help with event planning and setup
  • Mentoring: Guide students in test preparation
